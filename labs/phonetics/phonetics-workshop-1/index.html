Phonetics Workshop

In spelling bees, hearing the word correctly is half the battle. Let's practice distinguishing between similar sounds.

The 'Schwa' Sound

The schwa (…ô) is the most common sound in English. It sounds like 'uh' in 'about'.

Example: Bee buzzing (Actually a horse, using W3Schools test file)

Practice List

  1. Definition: Listen to the definition carefully.
  2. Origin: Ask for the language of origin.
  3. Sentence: "Please use the word in a sentence."

Pro Tip Always repeat the word back to the pronouncer to ensure you heard

it correctly!
